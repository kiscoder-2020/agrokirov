Sugar

Famously, a spoonful of sugar helps the medicine go down. But sugar is not just a sweetener. It also acts as a preservative, texture modifier, flavoring and coloring agent, fermentation substrate and bulking agent in a host of food technology applications.

XYZ is a top five global sugar merchandizer. We operate in 14 countries worldwide, with an extensive origination and destination network backed by excellent market intelligence and research capabilities.

As well as raw and refined sugar, we now offer a wide variety of sweetener solutions for a huge range of manufacturing and consumption purposes.

Sugar:

Beet Sugar ICUMSA 45

We buy white sugar made from sugar beet and export it to international markets.

Extra Fine Granulated Sugar

Our Extra Fine Granulated Sugar is an ideal all-purpose cane sugar made from the finest quality raw cane sugar grown. The cane is harvested and transported to our refining facilities, where it is refined to form the all-natural, pure cane sugar. It is white in color and has a sucrose content of 99.9%.

Brown Sugar

Our brown sugars are produced by combining cane granulated white sugar with molasses; creating a robust to mild cane molasses flavor. They vary in depth of color: Dark Brown Sugar Medium Brown Sugar Light Brown Sugar

Steviacane

We combine stevia and sugar as a single ingredient through our proprietary compound crystallization technology. During this process, cane sugar and stevia are interlocked to form a flavorful, shelf stable product. The result is a more consistent, uniform dispersion of sweetness and an improved sweetness profile over a simple blend of sugar and stevia.
